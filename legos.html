Build Things out of Legos!

Playing and building with legos is a great way to have fun and pass the time. Recently, I've been playing legos with my sister more and we've had a lot of fun. I recommend playing legos with your younger siblings because it can really make their day, and you may also end up having a lot of fun! The first three pictures are of my sister's legos, including her lego dragon. You can build things for fun, or you can try building more complicated things like castles or even a lego wall.
